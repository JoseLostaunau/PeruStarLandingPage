Room Description

Our basic room, perfect for a short visit to Lima. Every standard room has colorful linens, wooden floors, and a homey feel that differentiates us from other hotels. When walking to your room.

You will not find yourself staring down rows of similar corridors or opening the door to a room designed in corporate. Every standard room has been lovingly created by the hotel owner Lucy with the same care she brought to designing her children's rooms. Most standards also include a flowerbed connected to the windowsill and a terrace view.
